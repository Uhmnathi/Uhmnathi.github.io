About us

Convenient Training

Whether you want to hold in-person sessions for a whole class or grade over a time period or hold online training sessions, we have you covered. We can also work with you and your staff on-site or virtually to ensure the best learning environment for your teaching team. We do workshop tailored to your school's needs.

Highly Qualified Educators

We know that learning is easier when you have an excellent teacher. That's why most of our tutors have achieved an advanced degree in their field as well as boast track records of flying grades. Our instructors are passionate about the subjects they guide on and bring this enthusiasm into their tutorials and instruction materials.

Wide Variety of Courses

Whether you're looking to fill your child's gaps from class, assessment preparations or revision sessions, we can help. All of our courses, material and sessions are customizable for your needs to make sure you leave with relevant skills. We can make courses and learning material to address exactly the areas that hold you back from reaching your full potential.
